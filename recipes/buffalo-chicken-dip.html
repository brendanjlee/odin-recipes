Buffalo Chicken Dip

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Cream Cheese
  • Ranch Dressing
  • Hot Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Heat the chicken and hot sauce in a skillet
  2. Mix in the cream cheese and dressing
  3. Once the mixture is well-blended, add half of the cheese and stir
  4. Transfer the dip to a slow cooker
  5. Sprinkle remaining cheese on top and cook on Low until bubbly
